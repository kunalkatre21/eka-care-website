Frequently Asked Questions

What is Eka Scribe?

Eka Scribe is an AI-powered medical scribe that records doctor-patient conversations and auto-generates summaries, clinical notes, prescriptions, and structured EMR entries — directly into the Eka.care platform.

Is Eka Scribe HIPAA-Compliant?

Yes, Eka Scribe is designed with the highest standards of security and privacy, adhering to HIPAA guidelines to ensure all patient data is protected.

Can I use Eka Scribe during in-person or teleconsultations?

Absolutely. Eka Scribe is built for flexibility and works seamlessly for both in-person consultations and remote teleconsultations, capturing audio from any environment.

How much time can Eka Scribe actually save me?

Our users report saving over 12 hours per week on average. By automating clinical documentation, Eka Scribe reduces administrative work by up to 80%, freeing you to focus on your patients.
