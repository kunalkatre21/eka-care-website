## What is the full form of EMR and EHR in healthcare?

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/64d0bd8b475d468c8b1aa632/669a4ef88da0bd4bc0740f0b_plus.svg)![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/64d0bd8b475d468c8b1aa632/669a4f6bbc1f150ff8244c42_minus.svg)

EMR is an Electronic Medical Record and EHR is an Electronic Health Record.

## What is EHR?

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/64d0bd8b475d468c8b1aa632/669a4ef88da0bd4bc0740f0b_plus.svg)![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/64d0bd8b475d468c8b1aa632/669a4f6bbc1f150ff8244c42_minus.svg)

EHR (Electronic Health Record) is a digital record of an individual‚Äôs health information. An Electronic Health Record contains all the information about the patient‚Äôs past medical history, vital signs, progress notes, diagnoses, medications, immunization dates, allergies, lab data, and imaging reports. The digital record can also contain other relevant information, such as insurance details and demographic data.

## What is EMR?

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/64d0bd8b475d468c8b1aa632/669a4ef88da0bd4bc0740f0b_plus.svg)![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/64d0bd8b475d468c8b1aa632/669a4f6bbc1f150ff8244c42_minus.svg)

EMRs (Electronic Medical Records) are digital versions of prescriptions or paper charts used during a patient‚Äôs consultation. An EMR contains a patient‚Äôs treatment and medical history in one place.
