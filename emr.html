Frequently Asked Questions

What is EMR and EHR?

EMR is an Electronic Medical Record (a digital version of a patient's chart from one practice), while EHR is an Electronic Health Record (a more comprehensive report of a patient's overall health).

Why are doctors choosing Eka.care EMR?

Eka.care is a comprehensive, secure, and dependable clinic management platform. Features like AI assistance, WhatsApp integration, and customizable prescription pads help doctors save time and offer better care.

How is patient data secured?

All data is protected by encryption and we are FHIR compliant. OTP authentication ensures the doctor has complete control, and we sign a non-disclosure agreement (NDA) with all doctors.
